CRAFTING RECOMMENDATIONS FOR FILTER TAGS
================================================================================

RECOMMENDED BASES:

BLOOD CRAFTS (Ruby + Life/Steal Focus):
- Amulets: Any Magic Amulet > +10% Run/Walk, +Life after Kill, +Life
- Rings: Magic Ring > +Strength, Life Steal %, +Life
- Belts: Magic Belt > Open Wounds %, Life Steal %, +Life
- Gloves: Heavy Magic Gloves > +Strength, Open Wounds %, +Life
- Boots: Light Plated Magic Boots > +Life, Cold Resist, Life Steal
- Helms: Magic Helm > +Life, Life Steal %, Enhanced Defense
- Chests: Plate Mail Magic Armor > +Life, Life Steal, Enhanced Defense
- Shields: Spiked Magic Shield > +Life, Life Steal, Enhanced Defense
- Weapons: Any Melee (Ruby, Sol Rune) > +ED %, Life Steal %

CASTER CRAFTS (Amethyst + Mana/Cast Focus):
- Amulets: Magic Amulet > +FCR %, Regen Mana %, +Mana
- Rings: Magic Ring > +Energy, Mana per Kill, +Mana
- Belts: Light Magic Belt > +FCR %, Regen Mana %, +Mana
- Gloves: Leather Magic Gloves > Mana per Kill, +FCR %, +Mana
- Boots: Magic Boots > +FCR %, +Mana, Regen Mana %
- Helms: Magic Helm > +FCR %, +Mana, Regen Mana %
- Chests: Light Plate Magic Armor > +FCR %, +Mana, Regen Mana
- Shields: Small/Round Magic Shield > +FCR %, +Mana
- Weapons: Rods/Staffs/Scepters/Wands > +Spells, +Mana

HITPOWER CRAFTS (Sapphire + AR/Damage Focus):
- Amulets: Magic Amulet > +AR, +FHR %, Frost Nova
- Rings: Magic Ring > +Dexterity, +Max Damage, Frost Nova
- Belts: Heavy Magic Belt > Mana from Damage, +Max Damage, Defense
- Gloves: Chain Magic Gloves > +IAS %, +Max Damage, Defense
- Boots: Chain Magic Boots > +FHR %, +Max Damage, Defense
- Helms: Full Magic Helm > +AR, +FHR %, Frost Nova
- Chests: Field Plate Magic Armor > +AR, +Max Damage, Defense
- Shields: Gothic/Kite Magic Shield > +FHR %, Defense, Damage Reduction
- Weapons: Melee > +ED %, +IAS %

SAFETY CRAFTS (Emerald + Defense/Reduction Focus):
- Amulets: Magic Amulet > +Vitality, Damage Reduction, +Life
- Rings: Magic Ring > +Vitality, Damage Reduction, Poison Resist %
- Belts: Sash Magic Belt > +Enhanced Defense %, Poison Resist %, Reduction
- Gloves: Gauntlets Magic Gloves > +Enhanced Defense %, Cold Resist, Reduction
- Boots: Greaves Magic Boots > +Enhanced Defense %, Fire Resist %, Reduction
- Helms: Crown Magic Helm > +Enhanced Defense %, Resist %, Reduction
- Chests: Breast Plate Magic Armor > +Enhanced Defense %, Resist, Reduction
- Shields: Monarch/Dragon Magic Shield > +Enhanced Defense %, Resist, Reduction
- Weapons: Polearms/Spears > +ED %, Enhanced Defense

================================================================================

NOT RECOMMENDED:

CANNOT CRAFT:
- Circlets (any type - unique, rare, magic)
- Quivers (any type)

POOR CHOICES:
- ilvl 1-30 bases (only 40% for 1 affix, wasteful)
- Ethereal bases for casters (heavy penalty outweighs benefits)
- Low ilvl bases for endgame crafting (want ilvl 71+ for 4 affixes guaranteed)

================================================================================

ALVL TARGETS:

AFFIX DISTRIBUTION BY ILVL:
- ilvl 71+:     100% chance for 4 random affixes (BEST FOR CRAFTING)
- ilvl 51-70:   80% for 3 affixes, 20% for 4 affixes (GOOD)
- ilvl 31-50:   60% for 2 affixes, 20% each for 3-4 affixes (ACCEPTABLE)
- ilvl 1-30:    40% for 1 affix, 20% each for 2-4 affixes (AVOID)

KEY PREDETERMINED AFFIXES:
Blood: +Life, Life Steal %, Open Wounds %
Caster: +Mana, Faster Cast Rate %, Regenerate Mana %
Hitpower: +Attack Rating, Faster Hit Recovery %, Max Damage
Safety: Enhanced Defense %, Damage Reduction, Resistances %

================================================================================

GENERAL TIPS:

KEY CHANGES IN PD2:
- Flexible: Any magic/rare base of item type now works (was limited before)
- Exception: Circlets and Quivers still CANNOT be crafted
- Ethereal bases inherit ethereal property to result
- Rare bases can now be used, not just magic

CRAFTING STRATEGY:
1. Target ilvl 71+ bases to guarantee 4 random affixes
2. Use magic/rare base of craft-matching type
3. Combine with: Any Jewel + Perfect Gem (Ruby/Amethyst/Sapphire/Emerald) + Rune
4. Get predetermined affixes + up to 4 random affixes
5. Can upgrade crafted items like rare items
6. No inflated level requirements (fixed in PD2)

ADVANTAGES:
- Predictable predetermined affixes
- Flexible base selection (any of type)
- Upgradeable like rares
- Good for min-maxing builds
- Better than base rare drops when rolled well
